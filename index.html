01 — About

Decoding the Wheat Genome,
Advancing Crop Science

Dr. Suruchi Jindal is an Assistant Professor in Molecular Biology at the School of Biosciences and Bioengineering, Lovely Professional University, Phagwara. A recipient of the prestigious Young Scientist Award at the Punjab Science Congress 2017, she brings over 15 years of research experience in plant genomics and molecular breeding.

Her doctoral work at Punjab Agricultural University focused on the physical and genetic mapping of chromosome 2AL of hexaploid wheat — a foundational contribution to the International Wheat Genome Sequencing Consortium's landmark 2018 paper published in Science.

As a BIOCARe Women Scientist (Principal Investigator), she successfully led a project on mapping leaf and stripe rust resistance genes transferred from Triticum monococcum, combining high-throughput genomics, KASP assays, and transcriptome analysis.

She is also the Director of CropGenex, a bioservices company in Ludhiana offering genomic data analytics, statistical analysis, and scientific writing services.

02 — Research

Areas of Scientific Focus

🌾

Wheat Chromosome Mapping

Physical and genetic mapping of wheat chromosomes using HICF, NGS markers, SSR and gene-based markers. Contributed to the 2018 IWGSC reference genome (Science).

🛡️

Disease Resistance Genes

Mapping and characterization of leaf rust, stripe rust, and stem rust resistance genes in wheat, including transfer from wild relatives using molecular breeding approaches.

🧬

Omics Data Analysis

High-throughput GBS, SNP chips, WGS and RNAseq data analysis for SNV/Indel discovery, differential gene expression, QTL mapping, and transcriptome characterization.

🔬

Functional Genomics

Virus-Induced Gene Silencing (VIGS) of tillering (Max2) and dwarfing (PIN1) genes in wheat. Structural and functional analysis of auxin efflux carriers.

🌿

Crop Improvement via Wild Relatives

Utilization of Triticum monococcum and T. dicoccoides for introgression of beneficial traits including grain size and adult plant resistance into hexaploid wheat.

💻

Bioinformatics & AI in Agriculture

Application of machine learning and deep learning for rust disease classification, big genome data analytics, and statistical data analysis for genomic datasets.
